A Sacred Invitation

A Place of Prayer, Faith & Revival

At Elohim's Holy Fire Revival Ministry, we believe that a life touched by God can become a light to others.

Through prayer, the Word, faith, and the power of the Holy Spirit, we seek to encourage hearts, strengthen families, and awaken a deeper hunger for God. We are a community built on the foundation of Jesus Christ.

Whether you are seeking God for the first time, or you have walked with Him for years, you are welcome here. Come as you are. Let the fire of God meet you.

Meet the Leader

Meet Prophetess Sylvia

A woman of prayer, a vessel of the Holy Spirit, and a faithful shepherd of souls.

Prophetess Sylvia is the founder and leader of Elohim's Holy Fire Revival Ministry, based in Senya-Beraku. Called by God to carry the fire of revival to this generation, she leads with humility, passion, and an unwavering commitment to the Word of God.

Her life is marked by a deep devotion to prayer, a hunger for the presence of God, and a compassionate heart for people. Through her ministry, many have been encouraged, strengthened, and drawn into a closer walk with Jesus Christ.

"Today, I choose to do the work of God with joy, passion, and obedience."
