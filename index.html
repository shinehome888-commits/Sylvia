Meet the Leader

Meet Prophetess Sylvia

A woman of prayer, a vessel of the Holy Spirit, and a faithful shepherd of souls.

Prophetess Sylvia is the founder and leader of Elohim's Holy Fire Revival Ministry, based in Senya-Beraku. Called by God to carry the fire of revival to this generation, she leads with humility, passion, and an unwavering commitment to the Word of God.

Her life is marked by a deep devotion to prayer, a hunger for the presence of God, and a compassionate heart for people.

"Today, I choose to do the work of God with joy, passion, and obedience."
